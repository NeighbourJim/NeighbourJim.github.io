Projects

The following are a handful of projects I have worked on - Some as part of my Diploma, some personal for-fun projects.


GLEAMoscopeVR

Night Sky Education

A virtual reality application built in Unity targeting Android Mobile devices.
Built alongside a team of fellow classmates as part of my Diploma of Software Development.
Built for the International Centre for Radio Astronomy Research (ICRAR).
GLEAMoscopeVR allows the user to experience an immersive outback environment and view the night sky in a variety of wavelengths of energy, as well as observe various points of interest in the sky and receive narrated information about them.
Available on the Google Play Store here.

Lucid Reverie

Down the rabbit hole

A virtual reality experience created in Unity for the HTC Vive.
A short 2 minute experience that involved the player interacting with objects to complete puzzles with an Alice In Wonderland theme.
Developed with a team of fellow classmates and demoed at the Supanova Convention in Perth in April 2019.

Untitled Unity RPG Project

Totally Not Pokemon

A simple Pokemon clone RPG built in a few months as a part of my Diploma of Software Development.
Mostly focused on the turn-based battle system, with an overworld test area containing some wild encounters and one enemy NPC.
A fun project to work on that I'd like to return to and improve one day.

ShowBot

Dumb, Yet Fun

I maintain a personal Discord chat bot for a small (100~ users) Discord server.
The bot was forked from DougleyBot a few years ago, and I have heavily modified it since to add my own custom commands.
While the bot was primarily just for the fun of me and my friends, it also provided valuable learning experiences about asynchronous programming, as well as interacting with various APIs.
